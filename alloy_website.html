Less trauma. Faster recovery.

Minimal access surgery is done using small incisions and a camera to access internal organs.

Unlike traditional open surgery, where there is a large incision, minimally invasive surgery leads to less blood loss, reduced pain, smaller scars, shorter stays at the hospital and faster recovery.

METHODS OF MINIMAL ACCESS SURGERY

1

1

Laparoscopic Surgery

Performed by hand, using a laparoscope and a 2D Camera

Greater ability to access hard-to-reach places compared to open surgery (but less than Robotic)

Less costly when compared to Robotic surgery

2

2

Robotic Surgery

Performed by high-precision robots controlled by the surgeon at a console

Best ability to access hard-to-reach places

More expensive than Laparoscopic surgery
